1 Launch Briefing

Students rotate through five quick challenges. The goal is not just memorizing terms, but using vocabulary the way an operator or instrument technician would use it during troubleshooting.

Instructor Setup

Project this page or have students open it on laptops. Use teams of 2–3. Award points for correct answers and strong explanations.

Student Mission

Identify pressure terms, match definitions, interpret instrument situations, convert common units, and explain field readings.

Vocabulary Focus

Pressure, force, area, gauge pressure, absolute pressure, vacuum, differential pressure, manometer, transmitter, deadweight tester, hydrostatic head, span, range, and calibration.

Source context: ISBN 9780135213933 corresponds to Process Instrumentation, 2nd edition, by NAPTA, published by Pearson. This activity uses original, instructor-created review wording.
